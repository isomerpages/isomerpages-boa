### **Electronic Issuance of Practising Certificate from 2026**

To all Registered Architects <br/>

Registered architects who have applied for a Practising Certificate for the year 2026 and have fulfilled the renewal requirements will be issued an e-Certificate via email. <br/>

With effect from 2026, physical Practising Certificates will no longer be issued. <br/>
